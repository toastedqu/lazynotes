Computation operates at any temporal-spatial scale. It can be as small as a quark, as large as the observable universe, as short as the Planck time, as long as the cosmological decade.

Computation has 3 core properties:
- **Impermanence**
    - Computation means change. Change is dynamic.

- **Unity**
    - Every phenomenon is dependent on each other, either directly or indirectly.
    - The observable universe is merely a united computational process.
    - No phenomenon can exist with a self identity separable from other phenomena.
    - Hence, **no phenomenon exists in reality**.    
- **Non-dualism**
    - All dualistic labels are human concepts created & reinforced by computation throughout evolution due to survival instinct.
    - Computation just is. It has no dualistic label by nature.
    - Hence, **no attribute exists in reality**.

All 3 lead to the one and only true property - **Emptiness**.
- Every concept assumes a fixed, separate self identity by definition, violating all 3 core properties of computation.
- Hence, **no concept exists in reality**.
